# Lactase Activity Research Paper

A comprehensive research paper examining lactase enzyme activity, covering fundamental concepts, research background, problem identification, and study objectives. This academic document provides a structured analysis of lactase function and its biological significance through standard research paper sections.

## Project Overview

This repository contains a modular academic research paper exploring the lactase enzyme, its biological function, and its significance in human health and nutrition. The research paper is structured following standard academic conventions, with individual sections organized as separate documents for ease of development, review, and collaboration.

## Research Objectives

The primary objectives of this research paper are to:

- Examine the fundamental biochemistry and function of the lactase enzyme
- Review current scientific literature on lactase activity and lactose intolerance
- Identify key research problems and gaps in understanding
- Establish clear aims and objectives for future lactase-related research
- Analyze the broader significance and impact of lactase research
- Define the scope and limitations of the current study

## File Structure Explanation

### Root Level Files
